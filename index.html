Final Project

Welcome to my travel photography collection showcasing memorable destinations from around the world. This gallery features stunning locations across Asia and Europe, including the serene temples of Japan, historic European cities, and vibrant cultural sites in China and Singapore.

From the golden reflection of Kyoto's Temple of the Golden Pavilion to the ancient Leshan Giant Buddha in Sichuan, each photograph captures a unique moment in my journey. These images represent not just places visited, but experiences lived - whether wandering through Beijing's traditional Hutongs, exploring the artistic landscapes of Teshima, or strolling along the French Riviera in Nice.
